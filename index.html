About

     Rangawardhan is a 4 – Day Annual Marathi Extravaganza of VJTI Mumbai. Preserving the Marathi culture and adapting to the modernization, Rangawardhan in the past 14 years has carved a special niche for itself in everyone’s hearts. A festival awaited with much bated breath, Rangawardhan celebrates its 15th year with much pomp & show.

     Culture is the heart and soul of human life and hence preserving it is our utmost duty. Keeping this in mind Rangawardhan was founded in the year 2000 and has been celebrating the Marathi culture ever since. Rangawardhan has asserted the prominence of Marathi in art, literature and many such fields. While showcasing the Marathi culture through various competitions and being blessed with the presence of eminent personalities Rangawardhan tries to give something new and relevant each year.




Theme

     In a world torn by political, ethnic and religious conflict, only art has the power to unify, inspire, elevate, and heal . Art encourages us to view the world in new ways, as well as to look into ourselves.

     A world without art would be intellectually and spiritually barren. Imagination makes all things possible, and art is the most profound outgrowth of the human imagination that is not subservient to external purposes .Therefore, art is perhaps the purest phenomenon among human endeavours .

     Our theme 'Kalotsava' honours the various forms of art. Rangawardhan through its theme appreciate the major contribution of the art to the society. This year’s extravaganza will highlight the beauty of the art, especially the one displayed by our Marathi culture.
